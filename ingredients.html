Ingredients

We believe in transparency. Each Signature Scents fragrance is crafted using a blend of thoughtfully selected ingredients. Here's a breakdown of what's inside:

Midnight Ember

  • Alcohol Denat.
  • Fragrance (Parfum)
  • Essential Oil Blend (Smoky Cedar, Amber Resin, Patchouli)
  • Water (Aqua)
  • Benzyl Alcohol, Linalool, Coumarin*

Urban Spice

  • Alcohol Denat.
  • Fragrance (Parfum)
  • Essential Oil Blend (Black Pepper, Cardamom, Tonka Bean)
  • Water (Aqua)
  • Cinnamal, Eugenol, Linalool*

*Potential allergens listed as required by IFRA. All formulas are cruelty-free and phthalate-free.
